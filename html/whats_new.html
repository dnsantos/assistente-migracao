What's changing for you

Here's a visual guide to the main changes you'll notice after the migration.

Password reminder: After migration, your Mac password will sync with your Microsoft/corporate account. Avoid passwords with the following special characters: ~ ` ' " ^ — they may cause login issues. Very long passwords can also cause problems. Change yours at the Microsoft portal if needed.
New login with Jamf Connect
After migration, your next login will use Jamf Connect, which syncs your Mac password with your corporate account. Simply enter your corporate credentials as usual.
Tip: change your password before the next login if it contains the characters listed above.
How to request temporary admin access
  • Click the Jamf Connect icon in the menu bar (top of screen)
  • Select "Request Admin Privileges"
  • Enter the reason for your request
  • You'll have admin access for 5 minutes
This keeps your Mac secure while still letting you install what you need.
USB devices blocked
Unauthorized USB drives and external storage devices will be blocked automatically. An alert will appear on screen. Contact IT if you need access to a specific device.
This protects corporate data from unauthorized access.
Corporate wallpaper
Your Mac will automatically display the company wallpaper after migration. Custom wallpaper changes are disabled by policy.
The design may change with company campaigns.
