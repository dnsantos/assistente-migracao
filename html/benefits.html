Benefits of this migration

The new Mac management platform brings meaningful improvements to your daily workflow, security, and IT support experience.

Centralized management: Your Mac will be managed with a modern platform, ensuring automatic updates, configurations, and support.
Enhanced security: Better protection for your data, secure connections, and advanced threat prevention controls.
Higher productivity: Easier app installation, fewer interruptions, and better Apple feature integration.
Personalized experience: Dedicated support, policies designed for your organization, and continuous usability improvements.
Privacy guaranteed: No access to your personal data — focus is on protecting the corporate digital environment.
Transparent transition: The migration is communicated and supported by the IT team, with open channels for questions.
