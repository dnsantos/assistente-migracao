Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the MDM migration, Jamf Connect, USB restrictions, admin privileges, and security policies. Can't find what you need? Contact IT support.

How do I log in after the migration?
Login will be handled by Jamf Connect using your existing corporate credentials (same as Microsoft 365 / Outlook). If your password contains special characters ~ ` ' " ^ or is very long, change it before the next login to avoid sync issues.
I'm no longer an admin — how do I install apps?
Admin access is granted temporarily via Jamf Connect. Click the Jamf Connect icon in the menu bar, select "Request Admin Privileges", provide a reason, and you'll have admin access for 5 minutes. This keeps your Mac secure without blocking your work.
What happens if I plug in a USB drive?
Unauthorized USB storage devices are blocked automatically to protect corporate data. A notification will appear on screen. If you need access to a specific device for work purposes, contact IT to request an exception.
My wallpaper changed — can I change it back?
The default corporate wallpaper is set by policy and cannot be changed by users. This is a standard configuration managed by the IT team.
I forgot my password or can't log in. What do I do?
Your Mac password is synced with your Microsoft/corporate account. Reset it through the Microsoft portal (Office / Outlook settings), then restart your Mac. Jamf Connect will sync the new password automatically. Contact IT support if you're still locked out.
Will other restrictions affect my daily work?
The security policies are designed to protect the environment without impacting your normal workflow. Most restrictions apply to unauthorized external devices and high-risk system changes. If you encounter any issues, contact IT support.
Do I need to back up my files before the migration?
We recommend saving important files to OneDrive or another cloud storage solution before the migration as a precaution. Local files are not deleted during the migration, but a backup is always good practice.
Will my Mac restart during the migration?
The migration itself does not force a restart. After completion, Jamf Pro policies may request a restart to apply configurations. Follow the on-screen instructions if prompted.
